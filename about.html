A little about me

I was born and raised in New York City, and I am a proud alum of the NYC public school system! In high school, I was privileged enough to take classes in scientific research, exposing me pretty early on to not just fundamental research skills, but also the joy of exploration and learning. I was a huge physics nerd, hoping to eventually study it in college, but my AP Environmental Science class brought me to realize that I could be both intellectually satisfied and help to understand what I believe to be humanity's biggest issue: climate change.
